masked bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

A carina is a sharp ridge or raised line. The name comes from the Latin word for the keel of a ship.

Hylaeus (Pseudhylaeus) hypoleucus

    

Size
female 6 mm; male 5 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Found on Persoonia flowers, this species has a flat lower face and distinct carinae adjacent to the inner margin of the eye.

Distinctive Features
Other features are a thin pronotal collar with short white hair and white marks, plus an abdomen with translucent margins to the segments.

Flower Visiting Records

Persoonia spp.

Taxonomy

Phylum: Arthropoda
Class: Insecta
Order: Hymenoptera
Family: Colletidae
Genus: Hylaeus
Subgenus: Pseudhylaeus
Species: hypoleucus
