masked bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Hylaeus (Euprosopellus) certus

    

Size
female 9 mm; male 7 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Scutellum yellow; abdomen humped, slightly in female, strongly in male.

Distinctive Features
No yellow mark on collar; metanotum yellow; propodeum with single row of pits along front edge. The amount of yellow immediately above the clypeus of males varies.

Flower Visiting Records

Eucalyptus sp.Leptospermum sp.Callistemon sp.

Taxonomy

Phylum: Arthropoda
Class: Insecta
Order: Hymenoptera
Family: Colletidae
Genus: Hylaeus
Subgenus: Euprosopellus
Species: certus
