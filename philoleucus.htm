DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

The propodeum is the section of the body immediately in front of the constriction.

Hylaeus (Gnathoprosopoides) philoleucus

    

Size
female 4.5 mm; male 5 mm

Identifying Characteristics
Small black bees with short, blunt mandibles and white or pale cream face marks.

Distinctive Features
Distinguished from species like H. amiculinus, H. amiculiformis or H. amiculinus

not only by colour but also by the relatively smooth propodeum.
