masked bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Hylaeus (Prosopisteron) accipitris

    

Size
female 5 mm; male 4.5 mm

Identifying Characteristics
A very small species, mostly black. Female has a pale mark at least half the length of the clypeus. Male has cream face marks.

Distinctive Features
The male may or may not have pale marks on the pronotal collar and larger males have a curved ridge on the 3rd sternum. The distance between the antennal sockets is relatively small.

Flower Visiting Records

Grevillea laurifoliaLambertia formosaXanthorrhoea sp.
