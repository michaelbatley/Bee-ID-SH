masked bee

DETAILSDISTRIBUTION

Hylaeus (Rhodohylaeus) proximus

    

Size
female 5 mm; male 4 mm

Identifying Characteristics
A small bee with a red-brown abdomen, somewhat darker in the male. Much of the thorax is also red-brown in the female and the surface of the propodeum is coarsely sculptured in both sexes.

Distinctive Features
This species may be distinguished from H. lateralis by the smaller size and the absence of white marks from the collar.
